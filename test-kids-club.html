Kids' Club at Home

For Younger Kids:
We Can Worship God

Did you know God loves to spend time with us? One of the best ways to spend time with God is through worship. And guess what? You can worship God anywhere! Join Allison as we learn when and how to worship God. After you’ve watched the video, use these convo starters to talk about it.

Convo Starters
What is worship? How do we worship? And is it something we can do all the time?
What are the different ways we can worship?
How might you worship God when you're sad, scared or lonely.

For Older Kids:
Paper Airplanes & Diversity

Did you know God made us all different -- skin colors, talents, beliefs, and more on purpose? In the Tower of Babel, we find out how all the languages and ethnicities of today came to be. Join us and hear how God champions diversity and why we should also value different people. After you’ve watched the video, use these convo starters to talk about it.

Convo Starters
What is diversity? Why is it important?
Do people get treated unfairly because of their race, gender or religion? How can the Bible help us fight against that?
In school or sports or activities, how can we support each other's differences (in choices, beliefs and opinions) when we disagree?

What is kids' club?

A fun, welcoming, and safe experience designed just for kids to hear part of God’s Story in language they can understand. It’s free, totally secure, and offered during most weekend services at most sites. And don’t worry—we’ll let you know if your child needs you at any point during the service.

Built for all kids regardless of personality, energy level, or unique need. No matter their age, energy level, or personality, they’re welcome here.

Visit CrossroadsKidsClub.net

When is Kids' Club available?

Available during most services at most sites so kids can experience an environment designed with them in mind. View our location and service times before attending to make sure your kids can have fun with us!

Visit our locations

Is it safe?
Heck yeah!

Every volunteer that we work with has been background checked. All building exits are monitored by security. Each Kids’ Club room has a specific ratio of kids to volunteers so everyone is safe at all times.

Unique experiences for all ages

Experiences designed so a kid can be a kid. Every kid is unique and the same can be said about every age. Experiences for every age group are built to best serve them. We don’t have babies play dodge ball. Unless they’ve got great aim and then we put them in the fifth grade room. But just for one game.
