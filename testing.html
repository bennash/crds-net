Our camps are all about getting free.

Get off the grid, make lifelong friendships, and connect with God in a way you never have before.

OUR CAMPS

MAN CAMP
April 17-19, 2020

For: Men 18+

Sleep outside. Build fires. Laugh. Compete. Split wood. Drink malted beverages. BYO Pretty much everything. Test your strength. Tell stories. Make pals. Talk to God.

Learn more about Man Camp

WOMAN CAMP
April 24-26, 2020

For: Woman 18+

You were designed to be a strong force in this world—unleashed into your calling and operating in your gifting. Come fight for your freedom and walk into the fullness of who God created you to be.

Learn more about Woman Camp

Father/son CAMP
June 6-7, 2020

For: Fathers (or father-figures) and their sons (7–17)

Spark manhood in your son’s life. Ditch your phone. Play catch. Shoot guns. Spend time with God and brave your way through the woods. Fatherhood is meant to be an adventure.

Learn more about Father/Son Camp

Mother/Daughter Camp
June 13-14, 2020

For: Mothers (or mother-figures) and their daughters (7–17)

Kick back and relax for a life changing camping experience. Connect with your daughter in a way that everyday life doesn't allow. No chores. No dishes. No phones. God will be our host.

Learn more about Mother/Daughter Camp

MAN VETERAN CAMP
July 11-12, 2020

For: Men who are actively serving or have served in the U.S. Armed forces; as well as for men that have served in the military outside of the U.S.

Camaraderie. Spend time reflecting on honor and remembering sacrifice. Hear tales and laugh with tears. Hang with brothers. Get some shut-eye under an open sky. Find your new mission with God.

Learn more about Man Veteran Camp

COUPLES CAMP
October 16-18, 2020

For: Married Couples

The best things are often the toughest. Marriage is both wonderful and challenging. Come spend a couple nights in a tent and find some wide open space to stretch, grow and reconnect.

Learn more about Couples Camp
