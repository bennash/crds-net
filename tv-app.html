Crossroads TV APP

Bringing church to your living room, free.
Available on Roku, AppleTV and Amazon Firestick.

Get Inspired

You were made for more. More adventure, purpose, and fulfillment than where you are now. The Crossroads Weekly has new teachings every weekend to help you and your family breakthrough into the life God designed for you. Catch new episodes and binge past series all in one place.

Great for Kids

The App features tons of videos from our Kids' Club program, from animated bible stories to real-life tours of ancient Rome. Keep your kids busy with content they'll love that you can feel good about.

Worship at Home

Miss worship? Experience original songs, worship sets, and exclusive behind the scenes footage featuring Crossroads Music.

Get Back in the Habit

Our schedules have all been hijacked. The Crossroads TV App makes it easy to get back into a weekly rhythm. Download the App, pick a time, and bring your family together for church.

How To watch
